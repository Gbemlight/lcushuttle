## **DO's and DON'Ts**

### ✅ DO:
- Lead with **value** (safer students, direct sponsor access, operational data)
- Use **numbers** (50+ riders, 1.2K projected, 3x daily visibility)
- Show **trust** (verified drivers, school partnership, safety first)
- Ask **questions** ("How do you currently ensure student safety?" → Listen before pitching)
- Be **specific** (not "grow big" but "reach 1.2K monthly riders by month 3")

### ❌ DON'T:
- Talk about tech stack (React, Vite, Context API) unless asked
- Over-explain the MVP (it's a demo, not a thesis)
- Forget to ask for partnership/sponsorship next steps
- Minimize concerns (safety, liability) - address them directly
- Rush through sections - let people absorb the data
