## What Was Implemented

### ✅ 1. Brand Identity
- Added "LeadCity Shuttle" brand name
- Added tagline "Safe rides. Shared smart."
- Updated throughout navigation, hero, and footer

### ✅ 2. Trust & Safety Section
- 5-icon feature section
- Emphasizes verified drivers, fixed routes, student-only rides, 24/7 support, school partnership
- Placed prominently on landing page to address parent concerns

### ✅ 3. Who This Is For Section  
- 3 stakeholder cards (Students, Schools, Sponsors)
- Each with tailored benefits and value propositions
- Professional design with emoji indicators and checkmarks

### ✅ 4. Partner With Us Modal
- Replaces generic "Learn More" CTA
- Collects organization, email, phone
- Shows partnership info and direct contact details
- Fully functional contact form ready for backend integration

### ✅ 5. Business Impact Section
- 4 key metrics (50+ daily riders, 1.2K monthly, 3x daily visibility, 18-25 demographic)
- "View Full Business Case" button with comprehensive impact modal

### ✅ 6. Impact & Revenue Modal
- Detailed business case presentation
- Revenue opportunities (per-ride, sponsorship, data partnerships)
- Sponsor benefits (reach, frequency, demographics, cost efficiency)
- School partnership value proposition
- Professional design with actionable next steps

### ✅ 7. Booking History
- Shows user's recent bookings (mocked 3 rides this week)
- Demonstrates engagement and habit formation
- Key retention signal for investors

### ✅ 8. Enhanced Trip Information
- Added trip duration (~25 mins) to all trips
- Displays on booking cards for user clarity
- Practical information students need

### ✅ 9. Most Popular Trip Badge
- Zap icon + "Most Popular" badge on 8:30 AM trip
- Social proof element
- Draws attention to highest-demand slot

### ✅ 10. Enhanced State Messaging
- "Only X seats left!" (amber/urgent) when almost full
- "Try next schedule" (red) when full
- Urgency-driven copy improves conversions

### ✅ 11. Smooth Animations
- Progress bars animate smoothly during updates
- Modal fade-in animations
- Hover effects on interactive elements
- Professional feel throughout

### ✅ 12. Admin Dashboard Sponsor Metrics
- Business Metrics card: Average occupancy %, projected monthly riders
- Sponsor Insights card: Daily impressions, target demographics
- Translates operational data into business value

### ✅ 13. Operator View Footer Link
- Subtle link to /admin in footer
- Professional navigation for demo scenarios
- Avoids manual URL typing during presentations
